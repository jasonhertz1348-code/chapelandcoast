About the Studio

Sports design rooted in craft.

Chapel and Coast Creative is an independent graphic design studio specializing in sports. Based between Chapel Hill, NC and Charleston, SC, we work with teams, athletes, leagues, and sports brands who want visuals that match the intensity of the game.

Every project starts with listening — to your story, your fanbase, and your goals — and ends with design that feels unmistakably yours.

How It Works

Our Process

From first message to final files — here’s what working with Chapel and Coast Creative looks like.

01
💬

Discovery

Tell us about your project — your sport, your brand, your goals. The more context you share, the better the result. No brief is too small or too ambitious.

02
✏️

Concept

We get to work building your initial design direction. Every piece is created from scratch — no templates, no shortcuts. Just custom work built around your identity.

03
🔄

Revise

We refine based on your feedback until it’s exactly right. We don’t lock you in after one draft — we work until the design feels unmistakably yours.

04
📦

Deliver

Final files in every format you need — web-ready, print-ready, social-optimized. Fast turnaround so you’re never waiting on content when it matters most.
